About Connexta

Connexta is a smart university platform that brings students, faculty, and clubs together in one digital space for seamless communication and collaboration.

The platform features Notes sharing chat, AI-powered learning tools, and a comprehensive event system for creating, discovering, and managing campus events like workshops, fests, and seminars.

With smart event recommendations and easy participation tracking, Connexta enhances campus connectivity and makes academic collaboration more engaging.
