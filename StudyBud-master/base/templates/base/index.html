About Connexta

Connexta is a smart university chatroom and collaboration platform designed to bring students, faculty, and clubs together in one digital space. It simplifies communication, promotes idea-sharing, and fosters an interactive learning environment within universities.

The platform integrates real-time chat, file sharing, and AI-powered tools like a doubt-solving assistant and flashcard generator - all crafted to make academic collaboration seamless and engaging. Connexta ensures that every user experiences the power of connected learning through intuitive design and smart automation.

By combining innovation with simplicity, Connexta redefines campus connectivity, transforming the way students interact, learn, and grow in the digital era.
